Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Mind Motion Studios L.L.C. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Track My Flip application. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication metrics used when creating an account.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect background and foreground GPS location data exclusively for the purpose of tracking business mileage for your personal tax reports. This data is not sold to third parties.
  • Camera Data: Images captured to estimate item value are processed dynamically and are not stored permanently on our servers unless explicitly saved to your inventory.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data securely to provide our core services: plotting garage sales, tracking your business mileage for IRS reporting, and using AI to estimate resale values.

3. Data Deletion

You may request the complete deletion of your account and all associated cloud data at any time by navigating to Settings > Delete Account within the Track My Flip application, or by emailing our support team.
